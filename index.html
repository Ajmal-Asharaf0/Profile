About Me

As a recent graduate with a degree in Computer Application, I am a motivated cybersecurity enthusiast with a solid foundation in Vulnerability Assessment and Penetration Testing (VAPT), Security Operations Center (SOC) analysis, and proficiency in Python programming. My hands-on experience includes using OWASP ZAP for web application security testing, SIEM tools like Splunk for Security Information and Event Management, and Burp Suite for web vulnerability scanning. Dedicated to enhancing digital security through proactive measures and leveraging technology to mitigate cyber threats effectively. I am excited to begin my career in the field and contribute to the protection of vital information and assets. My academic background has equipped me with a solid foundation in network security, cryptography, and ethical hacking, and I am eager to apply these skills to real-world scenarios.

During my studies, I gained hands-on experience in security tools such as Wireshark, Metasploit, and Nmap, and I am comfortable working with programming languages such as Python and Java. I also completed several projects, including a vulnerability assessment of a local business network and a penetration testing exercise of a web application, which allowed me to develop my analytical and problem-solving skills.
